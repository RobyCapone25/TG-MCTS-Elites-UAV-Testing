# Security Policy

## Scope

TG-MCTS-Elites is a research prototype for simulation-based UAV testing. It is
not intended for direct control of real aircraft or as a safety-certification
system.

## Reporting a vulnerability

Please report security-sensitive issues privately to the authors:

- Roberto Capone: roberto.capone@mail.polimi.it
- Guglielmo Cattaneo: guglielmo1.cattaneo@mail.polimi.it

Include a concise description, affected files or workflows, reproduction steps,
and the potential impact. Do not publish credentials, private flight logs, or
sensitive infrastructure information in a public issue.
